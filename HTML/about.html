About Us


Happy Pill, founded in December 2021, is an online counseling platform, catering to the unique mental health needs of South Asians across the globe. We strive to create a safe space by allowing individuals to tell their story in a supportive & non-judgmental environment. Our Psychologists, Counselors & Therapists bring a diverse set of expertise, experience & skill sets to address concerns related to stress, anxiety, depression, trauma, bereavement as well as coping with relationship challenges, work-related issues, daily stressors and life transitions. We firmly believe that mental health therapy is universally beneficial to Anyone & Everyone. The uses of therapy extend far beyond addressing human crisis and tragedies; it enables individuals to tap into their core personality and wisdom to thrive.
